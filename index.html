Features

This drinking game is simple, but brilliant.

DRINK & GIVE

What would a drinking game be without these features?

With a correct guess, you can give a sip, while with an incorrect guess, you have to drink a sip of your drink.

If a player gets a card of the same value as any of his previous cards, the whole squad has to take a sip.

3 DIFFICULTY LEVELS

You can choose from 3 difficulties in the game:

- Warm Up

- Drinking

- Liver Damage

With each difficulty introducing new challenges to the game.

GUESS

At the start of the game, there are four face-down cards in front of each player. The task is terribly simple: moving clockwise, one guess awaits each player per turn.

1.: Will the first card be black or red?

2.: Will the second card be smaller or larger than the first card?

3.: Will the value of the third card fall between or outside of the values of the two previously flipped cards?

4.: What is the exact value of the fourth card?

BUS

The bus consists of a drinker and a dealer column. Going from the first row, we turn over the drinker and dealer cards.

On the bus, the number of seats (rows) is both the number of sips to be allocated and the number of sips to drink. If the value of a card on the bus equals the value of one of the cards in the players' hand, the player either has to drink or give sips.

The number of times a card is in that person's hand, you need to multiply the number of sips.
